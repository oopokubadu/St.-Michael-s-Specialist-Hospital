FAQ

Get Every Single Answer Here.

What are the visiting hours at St. Michael's Specialist Hospital?

Our visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM. We understand the importance of support from family and friends during your stay with us.

Does St. Michael's Specialist Hospital accept health insurance?

Yes, we accept various health insurance plans. Please contact our billing department for more information and to inquire about specific coverage.

How can I schedule an appointment at St. Michael's Specialist Hospital?

To schedule an appointment, you can either call our dedicated appointment line or use our convenient online booking system on our website. Our staff will assist you in finding a suitable time slot.

What medical specialties and services does St. Michael's Specialist Hospital offer?

We offer a wide range of medical specialties and services, including but not limited to general medicine, surgery, pediatrics, obstetrics and gynecology, orthopedics, and radiology. Our goal is to provide comprehensive care for all your healthcare needs.

What safety measures are in place at St. Michael's Specialist Hospital?

Your safety is our top priority. We have implemented strict infection control protocols, enhanced cleaning procedures, and follow all recommended guidelines to ensure a safe and secure environment for our patients and staff. Rest assured that you are in good hands.
